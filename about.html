Career

I also work at Advanced PCB as a Surface Mount Technician, where I assemble Printed Circuit Boards by populating them with electrical components using an SMT machine. Advanced PCB is a printed circuit board manufacturing and assembly facility located in Aurora CO, that specializes in fabricating PCBs from scratch and also populating them with chips and components. We work with many commercial, medical, aerospace, and military companies, so it is key that the boards we produce are built correctly so they can perform as intended in the field.
